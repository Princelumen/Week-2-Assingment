A BRIEF FACT ABOUT NIGERIA

Nigeria is located in West Africa and is the most populous country on the continent. It covers an area of about 923,768 square kilometers (356,669 square miles). as of 2024, Nigeria's population is estimated to be over 230 million people. the Capital city is Abuja, which officially became the capital in 1991, replacing Lagos. The official language English which was inherited from the colonial period under British Rule. Nigeria has a mixed economy with a large public sector. it is one of the world's largest producers of oil and gas, which plays a significant role in its economy.
